Colors

These are the predefined colors used throughout our patterns. Each color is displayed on various background colors for easy combinations. Those colors which have a yellow warning icon are not accessible combinations and should be avoided. For example, our grayscale below does not provide enough contrast on a white background (nor white on the gray background). Click any color for more information and developer values.
